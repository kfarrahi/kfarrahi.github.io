Overview

I am a lecturer in computing at Goldsmiths, University of London, UK. I am involved in the Data Science Masters program which will begin in October 2014.

I am interested in different topics related to data science, particularly machine learning, reality mining, and computational social science. Prior to joining Goldsmiths, I was a research assistant at Idiap Research Institute. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Swiss Federal Institutes of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL) in 2011.
